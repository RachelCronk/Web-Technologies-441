    • Majors/Prior Experience


    I'm majoring in Media arts with a focus in Video Game Design -- I wish I had had more classes in Game Design, but when I first enrolled in classes, that wasn't a big option at the time. I'm certainly making up for it now in my final few semesters. My prior experience in game design started around 2015 -- I have a few game jams under my belt, and had worked on several full-length games that were never completed due to a partnership falling out. Now, I am interested in learning how to make games as a solodev, and am very excited to pave my path forwards!

    Painting, Pixel Art & More!


    My hobbies are wide & varied! I am writing a novel series (with ever-expanding ideas on more stories and characters!) that is a dark fantasy / horror genre tale. I've been working on it for around 6 years, though the world it is in began to emerge in my mind around 2011. As previously mentioned, I dabbled in video game design for almost 10 years now -- I mostly focused on Pixel Art, however, and started coding last semester.

    I enjoy creating pixel art that is so detailed it doesn't look like pixel art! I try and take a painterly perspective when I make my pixel art, and most of my assets are around 256x256, and occassionally 1024x1024. I have experience in making tileables and assets that are 32x32 and 16x16, but have become more comfortable with the larger 128x128 and larger.

    I taught myself animation, and my time in UM has helped me further that knowledge! I am also a self-taught artist, and expanded upon that in UM as well! I paint--both traditionally and digitally. I use photoshop to paint! I lean towards landscapes and concept art. I also indulge in crafts -- I love to take old bottles and upcycle them into vases, and decopauge paper onto items too! I also enjoy Painting pots for houseplants!

    Videogames I play


    I really enjoy fantasy RPGs, and have since I was a little kid! It's hard to believe it, but I've been playing the Original Baldur's Gate 1&2 since I was 11 -- that's 21 years! Because of that experience, I have sought out fantasy games for most of my adulthood, ranging from TES Oblivion & Skyrim, Pillars of Eternity, Dragon Age, Neverwinter Nights, and just about any fantasy game I can get my hands on!

    Another formative title for me was Half-Life 2 in 2005 (more specifically, the orange box), which introduced me to FPS. Since then, I have enjoyed a variety of games such as DayZ, Fallout 3, Fallout New Vegas, Fallout 4, Sons of the Forest, Mass Effect, and more!

    I also enjoy indie games as well -- Rain World is the most recent Indie Game I've played that truly captured me!
